Your Wedding Experience

Mint Studios strives to create effortless, heartfelt moments for you and your loved ones on the biggest day of your lives. We believe that your wedding day should not only be the happiest day of your life — but the most seamless, sincere day as well. Soak in the wonderful memories as they happen, and let us take care of the rest. Your day shouldn't be spent worrying, it should be spent filled with joy and abiding in the overwhelming love of your friends and family.

Every moments is different, maybe it's the moment his eyes tear up when he sees you for the first time, or it's how your mother beams as your father hands off to your groom, or maybe it will be the way your best friend made you laugh when they raise a glass to you and your beloved. It's these unpredictable, wonderful moments, that make being a wedding photographer my passion. These little memories are each so unique and fleeting. They move so fast that try as you might, a few might slip through the cracks; but this is why we will be there, capturing every special moment for you, big and small alike. Because we will be photographing these memories for you, you won't have to grasp at one moment while missing the next — no happy tears forgotten, no long embraces lost — just beautiful moments to keep with you forever. Each memory perfectly preserved for you and your loved one to cherish and look back on with fondness and joy.
