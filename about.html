Meet our team!

Wynonna and Tiffany's philosophy is to create photos that will bring moments alive in your mind as they happened.

Co-Founder

Wynonna Moo

Hi! I'm Wynonna. I live in the heart of Vancouver, BC, with my golden, Sampson.
On your wedding day, yes, I am your photographer. But I also want to be someone you can trust, who you can lean on, and who makes your day all the more enjoyable and fun. I have zipped dresses, I have put on eyelashes, and I have touched up makeup, I have given hugs when needed.
My goal is to make you feel comfortable, beautiful, and cared about. I love relationships and friendships more than I love photography. The heart and soul behind MS is to come alongside you, love and serve you well, while also preserving memories from when your together began.

Co-Founder

Tiffany Tang

Hello! I'm Tiffany, but you can call me Tiff. I was also born and raised in Vancouver, BC. There is nothing I love more than pillow talk with my best friends and going on adventures.
You're getting married, and that is truly something to celebrate! I love all of the pretty details of the wedding day, but I also believe in the importance of the marriage that comes out of it.
My goal is to serve you wholeheartedly throughout your season of engagement, and to be a steady encouragement the whole way through! I hope that you come out of your JNP experience feeling fully cared for, and cherish the images that captured the start of your love story.
